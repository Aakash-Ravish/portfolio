Selected Work

Supply Chain Fulfilment Analysis
SQL · SQLite · Synthetic dataset
#sql#logistics#root-cause
Problem: A synthetic inventory/dispatch dataset (modeled on real warehouse fulfilment problems, not real company data) shows a 17.3% order shortfall rate.
Approach: 8 SQL queries (joins, CTEs) across inventory and dispatch tables testing three candidate root causes: supplier lead time, zone reconciliation lag, and weekday demand spikes.
Outcome: Confirmed two causes (supplier lead-time mismatch = 67% of shortfall units, Monday demand spikes = 9%) and ruled out the third — the data didn't support a zone-level effect.
